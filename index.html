About

I am a CEA Fellow and Research Director leading the MIND Team (40 researchers, 6 PIs) at NeuroSpin and Inria Saclay. My research spans over 25 years at the intersection of Applied Mathematics, Signal Processing, Medical Imaging (MRI, PET, MEG/EEG), and AI/Deep Learning for neuroimaging.

I am best known for co-developing SPARKLING, a multi-patented MRI technology that dramatically accelerates 2D/3D imaging on clinical 3T and ultra-high-field 7T scanners, and for directing deep learning reconstruction frameworks (XPDNet, NC-PDNet) that achieved a global top ranking in the FAIR & NYU fastMRI Brain Challenge (2020).

I am passionate about translating fundamental mathematical research into clinical impact — from epilepsy mapping and fMRI simulation to high-resolution MRI reconstruction with AI.
